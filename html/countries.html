ABOUT COUNTRIES

Turkey

Turkey is a country that connects Europe with the Middle East. Turkey has played a rather difficult role in history. A large number of wars took place on this land, which was accompanied by a change in the local population. More than two thousand ancient settlements are located on the territory of Turkey. The history of the peoples of Turkey began about 10 thousand years ago. An important role was played by the Greeks, who occupied this land in the XIV-XII centuries BC, their most important contribution was the development of trade and navigation. Later on the land of Turkey there were many colonies, and in the VIII century BC. e. the Phrygians and Libyans came here. Beginning from the VI century BC e., after multiple raids, the Persians began to rule. Today, rest in Turkey takes a leading position among tourist destinations ...

Egypt

Egypt is an amazing country shrouded in the secrets of the ancient world. There is hardly a person who, having seen the mysterious pyramids or the beautiful coral gardens of the Red Sea, will not be delighted. Going on a trip to Egypt, you can touch with your own hands the thousand-year-old tombs of the pharaohs who ruled Upper and Lower Egypt, visit the titanic ruins, the temples of Kornak and Luxor, as well as buy ancient and unusual gizmos.

UAE

The UAE is a deserts with blooming gardens, warm waters of the Persian Gulf, luxury hotels and many goods in stores. The United Arab Emirates is an opportunity to plunge into oriental exoticism, see picturesque landscapes, learn the cultural and national characteristics of an interesting country. In the days of nomadic tribes, the wealth of these places was only in golden sands and azure waters. With the advent of civilization and technological progress, it became clear that at the bottom of the ocean near the UAE there is "black gold" - oil, which is much more valuable than gold dunes. Thanks to the discovery of oil, this country began to develop very quickly and today a lot of tourists are striving to get to the Arab Emirates.

Spain

Spain is an amazing country that leaves the most vivid impressions for those who have visited it. It can be a trip to the coast or an excursion trip to historical sights, in any case, you will be satisfied. Spain is full of colorful holidays, delicious culinary dishes and the taste of young wine. Any time of the year, if you are bored, go to this country. The Spaniards know how to have fun from the heart and put on unforgettable performances. The best resorts in Spain are replete with good hotels that are ready to offer excellent service and plenty of entertainment.

Thailand

Thailand is a beautiful place with exotic plants and animals! If you have not traveled to this country yet, be sure to pay attention to it. Such wonderful resorts as Pattaya, Phuket, Nang, Kwang and others have been admiring tourists from all over the world for several years. The hottest hangouts and parties in Asia take place here. People come to Thailand to have fun in modern nightclubs, see unusual temples and visit interesting excursions, go shopping, taste original cuisine and learn the culture of the Thai people. Comfortable hotels are always happy to offer tourists excellent service and plenty of entertainment.

Czech

The Czech Republic is a large country saturated with romance, with amazing architectural monuments and beautiful landscapes. The country has favorable climatic conditions and good ecology. The Czech Republic attracts not only by its clean rivers and lakes, picturesque views of mountains and forests, interesting traditions, but also by historical treasures, the number of which exceeds 1500. These are ancient castles and palaces, austere parks and streets, pubs and taverns with a century-old history. In the Czech Republic, you can not only relax, but also undergo a course of treatment at the thermal springs, as well as go in for skiing.
